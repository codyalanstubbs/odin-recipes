Polly's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe

Description

My grandma Polly used to make this spaghetti sauce recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 - 1 quart jars of Ragu Spaghetti Sauce with meat
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 medium can tomato paste
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 6 pork chops
  • 0.5 pound hot italian sausage
  • 0.5 pound standard italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Bake chicken and pork chops @ 325F for two hours.
  2. Strip meat from bones - cut in large chunks.
  3. Strip sausage from casings - fry in bite size chunks (throw casing away).
  4. Add ground beef to fried sausage - fry until done.
  5. Add Ragu sauce to sausage and ground beef and stir mixture until homogenous.
  6. Add tomatoes and pork chops chunks and add sugar.
  7. Simmer on very slow rate for about 0.5 hour
  8. Ladle mixture over cooked spaghetti and enjoy!!
