Polly's Quiche Recipe

Description

My grandma Polly used to make this quiche recipe. It serves 4.

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs
  • 0.50 cup milk
  • 0.25 onions
  • 0.25 green peppers
  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Spray quiche dish with Pam.
  2. Bake for 0.5 hour or until brown and center is done. (Very Good)
  3. Make a salad and you have a good meal.
